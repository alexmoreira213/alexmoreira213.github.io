Project information

Trainline compares the costs of rail operators and tickets, which means you don’t need to. Find, book and pay for your tickets in time than it requires for you to reduce your patience. Based on our calculations, that is about 58 minutes.
By selecting to simply concentrate on selling tickets, we’ve made a decision to leave certain things apart. That means you will not be finding any advertisements, banners or messages that are annoying.
